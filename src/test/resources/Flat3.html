Description

The Space

The room is big. 20m2 in a villa with own entrance. The room has a 10m2 south-east directioned terrrass with views of the garden which you are also very welcome to use.

The price includes Wi-Fi,towells, cleaning, free parking in front of the house and bed linens.

We also offer bike rentals. The price is 50 DKK per day.

Guest Access

You will have your own entrance to the room and access to the small kitchen,. Bathroom and toilet are next to the kitchen and the room.

Interaction with Guests

We have contact with our guests respectly. We have had guests staying from almost the entire world: Europe, USA and 'Down Under'. Vi also rent out a second room.

The Neighborhood

The house is located very few metro stops from Nyhavn, Kongens Nytorv, Nørreport, The Blue Planet Aquarium, Copenhagen Airport, Christiania and Christianshavn.

Getting around

The house is located within metro, bus and bike distance from the center of Copenhagen. We rent out bikes for 50 DKK per day. Parking in front of the house is possible. The Copenhagen Airport is only three metro stops from the house. The ride is about 7 minutes and stops in Terminal 3. The Amager Strand Metro Station is approximately 500 metres ( 1600 feet) from the house. The metro runs 24/7.

Other Things to Note

Throughout the years we have had visitors from all over the world.

The house tells a very personal story. It was designed by Axel Breidahl who was a journalist and revue author during the start of the 20th century. He wrote the book "Ebberød Bank" and the song: "Sørens far har penge".
